Kitifo

Kitfo is a traditional Ethiopian dish that consists of minced or finely chopped raw meat, typically beef. The beef is often seasoned with spices and served with injera (a type of sourdough flatbread) or bread. There are different variations of Kitfo, and it is a popular dish in Ethiopian cuisine, especially during special occasions and celebrations.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : breakfast lunch dinner food

instructions

step 1

Finely mince lean beef, then season with mitmita (chili powder) and salt.

step 2

Melt spiced butter and mix it into the seasoned meat for richness and flavor.

step 3

Arrange Kitfo on a plate, garnish with extra mitmita, and serve with injera for a traditional Ethiopian experience.

ingredients

1 1/2 cups dry pancake mix

1/2 cup flax seed meal

1 cup skim milk
